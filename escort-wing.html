Escort Wing

01 Nov 2005

Escort a convoy of supply ships across hostile waters with your futuristic airborne fighter.

Will Hogben is primarily responsible for Escort Wing. I contributed about 10% of the contest code as well as the music. The original game was made for a three-month contest held by iDevGames in October 2005, but Will and an active community developed it further for a couple of years.

Escort Wing is written in BlitzMax, a BASIC-like layer over C++.
