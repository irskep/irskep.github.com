The New Dork Times

02 Aug 2013

The best writing of my life was produced in 1997 when I was eight years old, sitting at an old PC with my friend Eric Drury to write the New Dork Times. Although it ran for only nine issues published between 1997 and 1999, its biting social commentary remains as relevant as ever.

Earlier this year I came across my personal archives of all nine issues of the New Dork Times and decided they were worth two hours of attention to bring to a modern digital audience. I now present to you an online time capsule from 16 years ago, lovingly typeset in its original Times New Roman:

The New Dork Times – Digital Edition

I could only spare to hours from my normal archival duties of scanning bad comics I drew when I was 7, so please forgive some shortcuts I took with the layout and styling.
